How to use?

Use the calculator to perform basic arithmetic operations:

  • Click the numbers and operations to build your calculation
  • Press "=" or Enter to get the result
  • Press "C" or Escape to clear the calculator
  • View your calculation history below the display

Number Guessing Game Rules:

  • The computer will pick a random number
  • Enter your guess and press Enter or click Submit
  • The game will tell you if your guess is too high or too low
  • Try to guess the number in as few attempts as possible
  • Choose a difficulty level to adjust the range and number of attempts
